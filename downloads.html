Downloads

Please find below the latest TTK packages.

See our installation page for detailed installation instructions, our example database for end-user documentation and our Doxygen documentation for developers.

TTK is open-source (BSD license).
Feel free to use it at your convenience (for open-source or proprietary projects).

If you enjoy using TTK and find it useful, that'd be great if you could let us know by sending us an email at topology.tool.kit@gmail.com. Thanks for letting us know who you are and what you use TTK for. We would be excited to hear about your experience. Also, your feedback can help us improving TTK.

Note

TTK is currently mainly developed by academics. To convince our institutions to let us spend some time on TTK's development and maintenance, we need to provide them evidence of its usage. So if you enjoy using TTK, please consider citing the following technical reports in your technical documents or scientific publications:

The Topology ToolKit
J. Tierny, G. Favelier, J. Levine, C. Gueunet, M. Michaux.
IEEE Transactions on Visualization and Computer Graphics.
Proc. of IEEE VIS 2017.
